Recipe For The Best Lasagna You've Ever Tried

Some tasty steamy lasagna for you to enjoy with your family

This delicious layered italian cuisine is a perfect dinner for the whole family to enjoy.
Making it shouldn't be deterrering and by following this simple recipe you can be the households next Chef Bouyardee.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage (1 pound)
  • Lean ground beef (3/4 pound)
  • Minced onion (1/2 cup)
  • Garlic cloves (2, crushed)
  • Can crushed tomatoes (1 can, 28 ounces)
  • Canned tomato paste (2 cans, 6 ounces)
  • Canned tomato sauce (2 cans, 6.5 ounces)
  • Water (1/2 Cup)
  • White sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Dried basil leaves (1 1/2 teaspoons)
  • Fennel seeds (1/2 teaspoons)
  • Italian seasoning (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1 1/2 teaspoons, or to taste)
  • Ground black pepper (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Chopped fresh parsley (4 tablespoons)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese (16 ounces)
  • Egg
  • Sliced mozzarella cheese (3/4 pound)
  • Grated parmesan cheese(3/4 cup)

Directions

  1. In a dutch oven cook the following ingredients, sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until they are well browned.
    Stir in all of your tomato based ingredients with the 1/2 cup of water. Season this with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, italian seasoning, salt, pepper, parsley. Cover and let simmer for 1 1/2 hours with occasional stirring.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. After, drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
  3. Combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  4. Preheat oven to 375F
  5. Assemble lasagna in baking dish and bake for 25 minutes with foil covering on it, then an additional 25 minutes with the foil removed.
  6. Cool for 15 minutes and enjoy!
